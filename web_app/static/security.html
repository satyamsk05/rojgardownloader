Enterprise Security

Advanced Proxy Architecture

Security is the foundation of Rojger Downloader. When you initiate a download from platforms like YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or other supported sites, your request is routed through our advanced proxy architecture. This means your personal IP address is often shielded from the target server. By acting as a secure intermediary, Rojger Downloader protects you from malicious tracking, invasive advertising scripts, and IP logging by third-party content delivery networks.

Encrypted Streaming

All data transferred between our servers and your device is encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3 encryption over HTTPS. This ensures that your ISP, local network administrators, or potential eavesdroppers cannot intercept or monitor the media you are downloading. Whether you are downloading a public vlog or a private unlisted video URL that you have access to, the stream remains completely confidential.

Our proprietary Zero-Disk-Usage engine ensures that the physical AWS servers hosting Rojger Downloader never write your media to disk. The video bytes are piped directly into memory and streamed instantly to your browser. This means that even in the highly unlikely event of a server breach, there are absolutely no video files, databases, or logs for an attacker to steal. Your digital footprint is non-existent.

Infrastructure Integrity

The Rojger Downloader infrastructure is hosted on secure AWS EC2 instances, protected by strict firewall rules and continuous monitoring. We employ automated updates to our core extraction libraries (like yt-dlp) to ensure that we are always using the most secure, efficient, and bug-free versions available. We do not inject ads, malware, or unwanted toolbars into the downloaded files. What you request is exactly what you get: a pristine, bit-for-bit identical copy of the original HD video file.
